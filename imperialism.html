Imperialism

Imperialism happens when a stronger nation takes a weaker nation and dominates its religion, cultures.

Imperialism happens because people want to expand their land and have more people under control, also they needed materials to work and expand. There are five main imperialism causes: Economic, Exploratory, Ethnocentric, and Religious.

Imperialism was first practiced by European and Japanese nations in the 1800s and early 1900s. In any case he stronger nation would experience industrialization prior to practicing imperialism on a foreign nation or region.

Imperialism mostly happened in Africa and Asia but also in Europe. These are the countries that experienced Imperialism: England, France, Netherlands, Spain, Portugal, Russia, The U.S., Germany, Italy, Japan, Belgium and Turkey

Imperialism, especially American, can be still seen all over the world nowadays, despite the Age of Imperialism having ended in 1914!
